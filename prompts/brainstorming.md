# Brainstorming Prompt

Use this prompt to explore ideas and possibilities before committing to a direction.

---

## Context

> Describe the problem or opportunity you are exploring.

## Questions to Explore

1. Who is the target user or audience?
2. What pain points are being solved?
3. What existing solutions exist, and how do they fall short?
4. What makes this idea unique or valuable?
5. What are the riskiest assumptions?

## Output Format

- List at least 5 distinct ideas or directions.
- For each idea, note: **concept**, **target user**, **key benefit**, **biggest risk**.
- Highlight the top 2–3 ideas worth exploring further.
